Be a Good Samaritan, or find one

HelpStl is the good deed facilitator. We let locals tell their stories and ask the community for any kind of help or favor needed - donations for medical bills, a ride to a doctor's appointment, home or auto repair, literally anything. Compassionate donors/do-gooders answer the call when they're willing and able - when someone's story touches them. HelpStl captures unharnessed humanitarian potential and puts it to work on your block.
